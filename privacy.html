Privacy Policy for Instagram AI Assistant

Last Updated: October 2025

1. Information We Collect

Our Instagram bot collects and processes messages you send to provide automated responses. We collect:

  • Instagram user ID (for responding to your messages)
  • Message content (to generate appropriate responses)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Respond to your messages with AI-generated content
  • Improve our service quality

3. Data Storage

Messages are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored. We only retain message data temporarily for processing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API (for AI responses)
  • Meta/Instagram API (for message delivery)

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Stop using our service at any time
  • Request deletion of your data

7. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, please contact us through Instagram direct message.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of our service constitutes acceptance of any changes.
